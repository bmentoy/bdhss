In our Montessori program, we focus on nurturing the innate curiosity of young children through a self-directed learning approach.
Our classrooms are designed to foster independence and creativity with a range of hands-on activities that help develop fine motor skills, language, and early math concepts.
With experienced educators guiding each child at their own pace,
our Montessori environment cultivates a love for learning from an early age.
Our Lower Kindergarten (LKG) program emphasizes foundational skills in a structured yet playful setting. Here, children begin to explore basic literacy and numeracy through engaging activities and interactive play.
Our skilled teachers use a combination of storytelling, games, and artistic expression to build a strong base in language, mathematics, and social skills, ensuring each child is well-prepared for the next stage of their education.
In Upper Kindergarten (UKG), children continue to build on the skills acquired in LKG with more advanced concepts in literacy, numeracy, and problem-solving.
Our curriculum includes age-appropriate challenges that encourage critical thinking and creativity. With a focus on developing early reading and writing skills, as well as basic arithmetic, our experienced teachers ensure that each child is ready for a seamless transition to primary school.
Class 1 marks the beginning of formal primary education. Our curriculum is designed to introduce children to structured learning while maintaining an engaging and supportive environment.
We emphasize foundational skills in reading, writing, and mathematics, combined with exploration in science and social studies.
Our dedicated teachers use diverse teaching methods to accommodate various learning styles, helping each child build confidence and a love for learning.
In Class 2, students continue to develop their literacy and numeracy skills with a focus on enhancing reading comprehension and mathematical problem-solving.
Our curriculum incorporates hands-on activities and collaborative projects that make learning enjoyable and effective.
Our experienced teachers provide individualized support to ensure that each student masters essential skills and develops a solid academic foundation.
Class 3 introduces students to more complex concepts in literacy, mathematics, and science. Our curriculum encourages independent thinking and problem-solving through interactive lessons and projects.
We focus on strengthening students' critical thinking skills and their ability to apply knowledge in practical situations.
With a supportive and engaging learning environment, our skilled teachers guide each student to achieve their full potential.
In Class 4, students dive deeper into subjects like mathematics, science, and social studies.
Our curriculum is designed to challenge students and foster a deeper understanding of each subject.
We use a variety of teaching methods to cater to different learning styles, ensuring that every student has the opportunity to excel.
Our experienced teachers provide guidance and support to help students develop both academically and personally.
Class 5 focuses on preparing students for the transition to middle school. Our curriculum includes advanced topics in mathematics, science, and language arts, as well as social studies.
We emphasize the development of research and presentation skills through projects and presentations.
With our experienced teachers providing personalized support, students build the confidence and skills necessary for academic success in the years ahead.
In Class 6, students consolidate their knowledge and skills in preparation for more advanced studies.
Our curriculum covers a broad range of subjects, including advanced mathematics, science, language arts, and social studies. We encourage students to take an active role in their learning through independent research and group projects.
Our skilled educators ensure that each student is well-prepared for the challenges of middle school and beyond.

Secondary Education

  • Class-7 & 8
  • Class-9-10 English & Nepali
  • Class-9-10 Civil Engineering
Our secondary education starts from Class 7 and 8, where we focus on building a solid academic foundation and enhancing critical thinking skills.
Our curriculum covers core subjects such as Mathematics, Science, English, Social Studies, and Computer Science, along with optional subjects like Arts and Physical Education.
We emphasize interactive learning and encourage students to participate in various extracurricular activities to foster overall development.
Our experienced teachers use innovative teaching methods to ensure that each student understands and excels in their studies.
English Section:
Overview: This section offers a standard curriculum where all subjects are taught in English. Students benefit from a comprehensive education that prepares them for higher studies and global opportunities.
Subjects: Mathematics, Science, English, Social Studies, Computer Science, and Optional Subjects.
Highlights: Our teachers are experts in their fields, ensuring that students receive the best education possible. The focus is on developing strong communication skills and a deep understanding of the subjects.


Nepali Section:
Overview: This section provides the same standard curriculum as the English section but is taught in the Nepali language. This ensures that students who are more comfortable with Nepali can also excel.
Subjects: गणित (Mathematics), विज्ञान (Science), नेपाली (Nepali), सामाजिक अध्ययन (Social Studies), कम्प्युटर विज्ञान (Computer Science), and Optional Subjects.
Highlights: Our experienced teachers are adept at making complex concepts easy to understand in Nepali. This section helps preserve and promote the national language while providing quality education.
Civil section :
Overview: The Civil section is part of a special 9-12 program initiated by the government of Nepal to produce skilled youth. Classes are held in English, and students study a mix of compulsory and Civil engineering-related subjects.
Compulsory Subjects: Mathematics, Nepali, English, Optional Mathematics, and Science.
Civil Engineering Subjects: Students study four additional subjects related to Civil Engineering in both Class 9 and 10.
Highlights: This section is designed to provide practical knowledge and skills alongside academic education. Students get hands-on experience and are well-prepared to enter the workforce or pursue further studies in engineering.

High School

  • Commerce Stream
  • Science Stream
  • Civil 11-12
Commerce Stream:
Overview: The Commerce Stream provides students with a comprehensive understanding of business, economics, and accounting. This stream is designed for students interested in pursuing careers in business, finance, and management.
Subjects: Accounting, Business Studies, Economics, Mathematics, Computer Science, and Optional Subjects.
Highlights: Our experienced teachers provide students with practical knowledge and real-world applications of commerce concepts. The curriculum is designed to develop analytical and decision-making skills, preparing students for higher education and professional careers in commerce.
Science Stream:
Overview: The Science Stream offers an in-depth study of the sciences, preparing students for careers in engineering, medicine, and research. This stream focuses on developing strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, Computer Science, and Optional Subjects.
Highlights: Our science faculty comprises highly qualified and experienced teachers who ensure that students gain a deep understanding of scientific principles. Students have access to well-equipped laboratories for hands-on experiments and research projects.
Civil 11-12:
Overview: The Civil Stream is part of the government's 9-12 program, aimed at producing skilled professionals in civil engineering. This stream combines academic education with practical training in civil engineering concepts and practices.
Compulsory Subjects: Mathematics, Nepali, English, Optional Mathematics, and Science.
Civil Engineering Subjects: In addition to the compulsory subjects, students study four civil engineering-related subjects, gaining hands-on experience in the field.
Highlights: This program equips students with the knowledge and skills required for careers in civil engineering. Our curriculum is designed to meet industry standards, and students benefit from practical training and project-based learning.

Diploma

  • Diploma in Civil-Engineering (CTEVT)
Diploma in Civil Engineering (CTEVT):
Overview: This diploma program, affiliated with the Council for Technical Education and Vocational Training (CTEVT), focuses on producing skilled civil engineering technicians. The curriculum is designed to provide a blend of theoretical knowledge and practical skills.
Subjects: Core subjects include Engineering Drawing, Building Construction, Structural Engineering, Surveying, Hydraulics, and Soil Mechanics. Additionally, students undertake practical projects and internships.
Highlights: Our experienced faculty, state-of-the-art laboratories, and industry partnerships ensure that students receive hands-on training and real-world experience. The program prepares students for immediate employment in the civil engineering sector or for further studies in engineering.
